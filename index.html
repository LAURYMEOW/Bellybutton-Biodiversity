Curious fact

Belly buttons are very diverse habitats! Referring to the article "Belly Button Biodiversity: Citizen scientists navigate'the intimate forests of the umbilicus.'" published by National Geographic, in total, have been discovered 2,368 different species. More than half of those may be new to science.(1)
